Turtley the wise turtle

Home Back to Stories

Turtley the wise turtle lived in a pond in the forest. He was the oldest turtle in the pond, and all the other turtles would come to him for advice. One day, a young turtle came to Turtley and asked him, "How can I be wise?" Turtley smiled and said, "To be wise, you must learn from your experiences and the experiences of others. You must be patient and thoughtful. You must also be kind and compassionate." The young turtle thanked Turtley for his advice and went on his way. He followed Turtley's advice, and over time, he became a wise and respected turtle himself.
